Smart Photo Selection

AI automatically picks the best photos from Facebook

How It Works

Step 1: Fetch Photos

Every day, we pull recent photos from our Facebook page.

Step 2: AI Analysis

Amazon's AI (Rekognition) analyzes each photo looking for:

  • 3 or more people in the photo
  • At least one person smiling
  • No text overlays (rejects slides/graphics)

Step 3: Smart Cropping

Photos are automatically cropped to position faces at the optimal spot.

Step 4: Optimization

Images are compressed and converted to modern formats for fast loading.

Result: The hero slider always has fresh, engaging photos of our congregation - no manual work required!

Featured Events

The yellow "Hello Bar" that appears on every page

How to Feature an Event

In Planning Center Calendar:

  1. Open the event you want to feature
  2. Click the star icon to mark as "Featured"
  3. Add a header image for best results
  4. Add a description

What Happens:

  • Yellow banner appears on every page
  • Event gets its own dedicated page
  • Automatically removed after event date

Best Practices

  • Only feature ONE event at a time
  • Use for special events, not regular services
  • Add an eye-catching header image
  • Include clear event details
